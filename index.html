We are Kinzie Dog People!

We need your help!

In an effort to keep our neighborhood beautiful, we seek to petition 27th Ward Alderman Walter Burnett Jr. to have garbage cans installed along the Kinzie Street greenway.

Local dog owners greatly appreciate this green space, and we would like to keep the area clean and enjoyable by adding garbage cans in two strategic situations.

With your support, we can make this happen. We will present a list to Aldmerman Burnett on April 1, 2014.

Your email address will not be used for any purpose other than presenting this list. If you choose, you may select to be informed about progress and other neighborhood initiatives. The list including your name - but not your email - will be available on this website.
